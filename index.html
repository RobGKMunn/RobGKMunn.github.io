Rob Munn

Biography

Rob is a lecturer in the Discipline of Pharmacology and Therapeutics at the National University of Ireland, Galway (NUIG), taking up this position in February 2020. Prior to this appointment, he was a Postdoctoral Scholar in the Laboratory of Lisa Giocomo at Stanford University. He earned his PhD in the lab of David Bilkey at the University of Otago, in New Zealand, in 2012.
