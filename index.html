ABOUT ME

I am a Neuroscientist

I am an in vivo electrophysiologist whose interests are uncovering the bases behind learning and memory. I study the hippocampal-entorhinal loop using a variety of tools and techniques in an attempt to understand the neural substrates of memory. I recieved my undergraduate and graduate training at the University of Otago in New Zealand before coming to Stanford University to take a Postdoctoral position in the lab of Dr. Lisa Giocomo.
